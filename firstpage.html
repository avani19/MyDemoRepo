HOW TO MAKE A SANDWICH?

Below are the steps to make a SANDWICH

  1. Take the packet of bread. open it and get the two pieces of bread.
  2. Spread mayo on one side of each bread.
  3. Put pice of tomato, spinach, lettuce, cucumber and CHEESE over one pice of bread.
  4. Put the other pice of bread on the top of the cheese.
  5. You may put chips on the side as a side dish.

Sandwich is ready to eat
